What is SCLC?

The AIS Student Chapter Leadership Conference is an annual national event where information systems students connect, learn, and compete in technology-driven challenges. Hosted at Temple University in 2027, SCLC is where the next generation of MIS leaders comes to prove themselves.

Leadership Development
Team-based competitions, workshops, and mentorship from industry leaders.
Tech Competitions
Real-world challenges in AI, FinTech, and cybersecurity judged by industry pros.
Professional Networking
250+ students connect directly with recruiters and executives hiring MIS talent.
National Reach
Students from MIS programs across the country at one premier gathering.

Why sponsor SCLC?

Your choice to sponsor SCLC 2027 is not just a donation, but rather a partnership with the strongest MIS students in the country. Whether your goal is hiring, brand visibility, thought leadership, or community investment, SCLC delivers measurable value across one and a half days, and a year-round relationship with MIS at Temple University.

Talent Pipeline

Interact with up to 250+ pre-qualified MIS students actively entering the workforce, with priority resume access and private networking sessions.

Brand Visibility

Logo placement, co-branding opportunities, and stage presence in front of the top MIS students from programs across the country.

Thought Leadership

Panel seats, keynote slots, and workshop opportunities that position your leaders as voices shaping the next generation of MIS professionals.

Community Investment

A meaningful partnership with Temple Fox MIS that extends beyond the conference into year-round student development and campus engagement.

Help us make SCLC 2027 unforgettable
